Chuy's Creamy Jalapeno Dip

Description

Destined to be a party favorite! This will be THE dip friends and family ask you to bring on repeat. Luckily for you, this is a "dump in the blender and go" recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup mild tomatillo salsa
  • 1 handful cilantro
  • 1 dry ranch dressing packet
  • 1/4 cup pickled jalapenos

Steps

  1. Toss all ingredients into a food processor and blend until smooth.
  2. Taste and adjust as desired.
